Health

It is a state of complete complementarity between body, mind and soul, and the concept of health is not limited to the mere absence of disease or disability, It also includes the ability to perform everyday activities normally and enjoy life fully


Health departments

  • Physical health

    It is the health condition of the bodt, which includes internal organs, muscles, bones and skin

    1. Physical safety
    2. Fitness
    3. Reproductive health

  • Mental health

    It is the state of health of the mind, and includes emotions, thoughts and behaviors

    1. Psychological health
    2. Emotional health

  • Social health

    It is the state of health of social relations, and includes relationship with family, friends and society

    1. Social relationships
    2. Social participation
    3. Environmental health




Importance of health

Health is of great importance in human life, it is the foundation of everything in life, good health allows human beings enjoy life fully, and It helps him to discharge his duties and responsibilities fully



Causes of deterioration of health

  1. Genetic factors
  2. Environmental factors
  3. Behaviuoral factors

Ways to maintain health

  1. Health diet
  2. Exercise regularly
  3. Getting enough sleep
  4. Keep away from smoking and alcohol
  5. Control stress
